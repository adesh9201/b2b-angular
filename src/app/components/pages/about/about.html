About FabHub

Connecting the world's fabric industry through innovative B2B marketplace solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FabHub emerged from a simple vision: to revolutionize how the global fabric industry connects, trades, and grows together.

We recognized the challenges faced by fabric manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers in finding reliable partners, managing complex supply chains, and accessing global markets. Our platform was built to solve these pain points through technology, transparency, and trust.

Today, FabHub serves as the premier B2B marketplace for the fabric industry, connecting thousands of businesses across continents and facilitating millions of dollars in trade annually.

Our Mission

To empower fabric businesses worldwide by providing a seamless, transparent, and efficient B2B marketplace that fosters growth, innovation, and sustainable partnerships in the global textile industry.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading B2B fabric marketplace, where every fabric business can discover new opportunities, build lasting partnerships, and thrive in an increasingly connected global economy.
